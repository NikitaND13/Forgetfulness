F O R G E T F U L N E S S

The name of the author is the first to go

followed obediently by the title,

the plot, the heartbreaking conclusion,

the entire novel which suddenly becomes one you have never read, never even heard of,

as if, one by one, the memories you used to harbor decided to retire

to the southern hemisphere of the brain,to a little fishing village where there are no phones.

Whatever it is you are struggling to remember it is not poised on the tip of your tongue,

not even lurking in some obscure corner of your spleen.

It has floated away down a dark mythological river whose name begins with an L
as far as you can recall,

well on your own way to oblivion where you will join those

who have even forgotten how to swim and how to ride a bicycle.
